Beyond Vulnerable

A concept | 2019

BeyondVulnerable is an application which empowers users suffering from anxiety disorders. 

Globally, 1 in 13 people suffer from anxiety. It is difficult for a patient to manage an anxiety attack in a social setting or when they are alone.  One of the most effective tool which has been proven to help manage stress and calm down is listening to calming sounds and focusing on breathing.

BeyondVulnerable will guide you through breathing exercises and help calm down via personalized calming voice tracks.



Problem

There is a prevalent stigma around mental health. Moreover, despite the number of people seeking professional help from psychologists, they are not able to cope up with anxiety disorders on their own as there are unpredictable triggers to these episodes.

Goal

Provide people with anxiety disorders with an effective solution to calm down during stressful situations so that they can cope better on their own.

Role

UX Designer and Strategist

  • A user-centered design process was followed beginning with user interviews with 15 acquaintances with 6 of them seeking professional help for anxiety disorders. 
  • A contextual inquiry was included to know the problems people faced while in a stressful situation in social settings and what they try to do to overcome those problems.
  • Songs like Weightless by Marconi Union have been proven to reduce anxiety by 65%.
  • Furthermore, experiments in psychology have revealed that therapy sessions are twice as effective when patients are spoken with in their mother tongue.
  • Placing these findings at the center of the solution and integrating it with technology, BeyondVulnerable creates a safe personal space for users while aiding them in relaxing.

The Process


    Deliverables of BeyondVulnerable:

  • User can choose from a range of sound options (Music, Nature, Human)
  • The Human section under the Calm Me tab will contain voice recordings guiding the user with breathing exercises and giving necessary assurance thus making the user feel that they are not alone. 
  • In the Human section under the Calm Me tab, language selection and gender selection gives the user the freedom to choose their mother tongue as a language for communication.
  • Users can read about other means to manage an anxiety attack or panic attack. This can help in informing users to better assist others going through the same disorders.
  • The ‘Get Help’ page helps the user find the nearest psychologist based on user’s location to help them with anxiety disorders.
