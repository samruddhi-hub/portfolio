Deli Carton

Branding & Social Media

Deli Carton is a grocery delivery service based in Thane, Maharashtra. Currently operating via social media, they are expected to launch a website and mobile application soon.

We went for tones of brown since it evokes a sense of strength and reliability.
Brown is seen as something solid, much like the earth, and it's a color often associated with dependability, security, and safety.

"Deli" is a wordplay on the term 'daily' to imply the day-to-day items that we use.
"Carton" of course stood for the concept of a delivery service.



Being a grocery delivery service, Deli Carton wanted the brand to convey feelings of exemplary quality, a dependable source of daily products and prompt service.

This journey of ideating and iterating led us to what is now the logo for Deli Carton.



When we thought about Deli Carton and what we wanted our brand to be about, we thought about 'modern' and 'trustworthy' while not being very formal.
Montserrat felt like it was cut out for our brand.
The perfect large x-height that we wanted, since the letters were going to be lower cased.
It felt right, it felt like home just the way Deli Carton is supposed to feel.
